VANDE MATARAM

vandēmātaraṃ sujalāṃ suphalāṃ malayaja śītalāṃ sasya śyāmalāṃ mātaraṃ ॥vandē॥
śubhrajyōtsnā pulakitayāminīṃ pullakusumita drumadala śōbhinīṃ suhāsinīṃ sumadhura bhāṣiṇīṃ sukhadāṃ varadāṃ mātaraṃ ॥ vandē ॥
kōṭikōṭi kaṇṭha kalakala ninādakarālē kōṭi kōṭi bhujair dhṛta kara karavālē abalā kēyanō mā ētō balē bahubala dhāriṇīṃ namāmi tāriṇīṃ ripudalavāriṇīṃ mātarām ॥ vandē ॥
timi vidyā timi dharma tumi hṛdi tumi marma tvaṃ hi prāṇāḥ śarīrē bāhutē tumi mā śakti hṛdayē tumi mā bhakti tō mārayi pratimā gaḍi mandirē mandirē ॥ vandē ॥
tvaṃ hi durgā daśa praharaṇa dhāriṇī kamalā kamaladaḻa vihāriṇī vāṇī vidyādāyinī namāmi tvāṃ namāmi kamalāṃ amalāṃ atulāṃ sujalāṃ suphalāṃ mātaram ॥ vandē ॥
śyāmalāṃ saralāṃ susmitāṃ bhūṣitāṃ dharaṇīṃ bharaṇīṃ mātaraṃ
